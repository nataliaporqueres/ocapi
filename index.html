About

The OCAPi project develops an advanced pixel-level framework for optimally extracting information from weak lensing data, particularly from the Euclid mission and the Rubin Observatory. By performing inference directly at the field level, OCAPi aims to deepen our understanding of cosmic structure formation and the nature of dark energy.

Team

  • Natalia Porqueres (PI)

Collaborations

OCAPi is closely linked to international cosmology efforts, including:

  • HOWLS: High Order Weak Lensing Statistics collaboration
  • DESC: Dark Energy Science Collaboration (Rubin Observatory)
  • Euclid Mission: European Space Agency’s cosmology mission

These collaborations foster the exchange of methods and data for next-generation cosmological inference.
