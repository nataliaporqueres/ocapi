About

The OCAPi project develops an advanced pixel-level framework for optimally extracting information from weak lensing data, particularly from the Euclid mission and the Rubin Observatory. By performing inference directly at the field level, OCAPi aims to deepen our understanding of cosmic structure formation and the nature of dark energy.
