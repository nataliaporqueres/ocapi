About

The OCAPi project develops an advanced pixel-level framework for optimally extracting information from weak lensing data, particularly from the Euclid mission and the Rubin Observatory. Its goal is to enhance our understanding of dark energy and cosmic structure formation by analyzing data at the field level using novel inference techniques.

Work Packages

  • WP1: Redshift distribution and data contamination
  • WP2: Scalability and computational efficiency
  • WP3: Pixel-level analysis of Euclid weak lensing data
  • WP4: Joint analysis of galaxy clustering and weak lensing

Collaborations

OCAPi is closely tied to the HOWLS and DESC collaborations, and contributes to the Euclid mission through advanced forward-modelling and field-level inference efforts.
